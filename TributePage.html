  • 1896 - Gets into college. Never attend lectures, Teachers hate him.
  • 1900 - Graduates with a degree in maths and physics
  • 1905 - Age 26. Comes up with Quantum theory of light, Proved the existence of atoms, Special theory of relativity
  • 1908 - Gets his Doctorate
  • 1922 - Noble Prize in physics for discovering the photoelctric effect.
